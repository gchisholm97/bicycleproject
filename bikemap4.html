Bicycle Traffic Density in Chicago, IL

This map visualizes the rental bicycle traffic in Chicago, IL on July 4th, 2024. Using start and end location data from Divvy Bikes, assumed individual ride route geometries were derived using Mapbox's Directions API. By temporally interpolation each rider's position along their route using Turf.js, the map reveales how the urban peloton evolved throughout the day.

Users can use the player console to scroll through the day, and find out where Chicagoans rode Divvy Bikes to soak up the sun on the 4th of July, 2024.
